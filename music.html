Musical Compositions, Arrangements, and Sketches

Feel free to print and play any of these at your leisure (and let me know what you think). All of my pieces are under a Creative Commons License as outlined at the bottom of this page. They are organised by the following sections: Original Work, Arrangements, and Sketches; each of which should be fairly self explanatory. Each piece has an mp3, pdf, and Musescore file available.

Original Work

Something Nasty

This is short (1 minute) string quartet written for my Music Theory II class at Purdue.

2 Violin Viola Cello

The Downfall

A thematic piece for solo piano. It features chromatic motifs to convey a shift in musical plot.

Piano

The Cold of Night

A chamber piece featuring a violin, viola, piano. Progressing from the calm and peaceful beginning section, the piece evolves into a more aggresive setting from which it resolves.

Violin Viola Piano

Arrangements

HoneyBee by Steam Powered Giraffe

Steam Powered Giraffe is a musical sketch comedy group that started in 2008 in San Diego. Their performance of this song in particular features lots of accoustic sounds and multiple part harmonies. Taking the primary rhythm of the song and the leading voice of the melody, I created a arrangement for a voice and piano accompaniment.

Voice Piano

Sketches

Muse

Piano

Hushwing Gryff

This theme was inspired by the artwork for the Magic: the Gathering card of the same name by John Severin Brassell. Just as "an overwhelming sense of calm accompanies the gryffs that wheel above the roofs of Gavony," I also aimed to inspire a sense of calm with the primary motifs in this piece.

Piano

A short dance theme most likely inspired from working on my project for my Music Theory II class.
